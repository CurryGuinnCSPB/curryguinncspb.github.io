The projects below were completed by students in the CU Boulder Applied Computer Science Post-Baccalaureate program. This section is intended to showcase the range of work students produce in courses such as Natural Language Processing, Data Structures, and Professional Development.

This initial build includes the NLP collection. The landing page is organized in three layers:

1. featured projects
2. category browsing
3. a complete archive of all NLP projects
